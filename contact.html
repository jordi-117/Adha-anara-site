Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour?

You can book directly via WhatsApp or by filling out the form above. We'll confirm availability and send you all the details within 24 hours.

What is included in the tour?

All tours include a professional guide, binoculars if needed, and a species checklist. Transportation and meals are not included unless specified.

Do I need experience in birdwatching?

Not at all! Our tours are designed for all levels, from complete beginners to experienced birders. Our guides adapt to every group.

What is the best time to visit?

Our routes operate year-round. However, the dry seasons (December–February and June–August) generally offer the best conditions for birdwatching.
