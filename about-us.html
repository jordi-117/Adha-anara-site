About Us

Our Story

Adha Anara Birdwatching was born from a deep love for the birds and landscapes of San Agustín, Huila. Founded by a team passionate about nature and conservation, we set out to share the incredible biodiversity of the Colombian Massif with visitors from around the world. Our name reflects our connection to the land and its wildlife — every tour we lead is a celebration of life in its most colorful and musical form.

Our Mission

To provide authentic, educational, and responsible birdwatching experiences that connect people with the natural heritage of San Agustín while supporting local conservation and community development.

Our Vision

To become a leading birdwatching destination in South America, recognized for the quality of our guides, the depth of our ecological knowledge, and our commitment to sustainable tourism in the Colombian Massif.

Our Commitment to Conservation

Conservation is at the heart of everything we do. We work closely with local communities to promote the protection of natural habitats and bird populations. A portion of every tour fee goes directly toward conservation initiatives in the Colombian Massif, supporting habitat restoration, species monitoring, and environmental education programs.

Habitat Protection

We actively support local conservation areas and promote responsible access to sensitive ecosystems.

Community Involvement

Local communities are our partners. We hire locally, source locally, and ensure tourism benefits reach those who live alongside the birds.

What Makes Us Different

Expert Local Guides

Our guides have spent years learning every trail, season, and species in the region — knowledge no guidebook can replicate.

Passion-Driven

We don't just run tours — we share a lifestyle. Every outing reflects our genuine love for birds and wild places.

Photography Focused

We know how to position you for the perfect shot. Our routes are selected with both birders and photographers in mind.

Small Groups

We keep our groups small (max 6 people) to minimize environmental impact and maximize your personal experience.
